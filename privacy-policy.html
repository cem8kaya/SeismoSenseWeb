Privacy Policy

How SeismoSense handles your data — and why the answer is mostly "it doesn't."

Last updated: February 27, 2026

The short version: SeismoSense processes all seismic data entirely on your device. No seismic readings, sensor data, or location information is ever transmitted to our servers or any third party. Your data is yours.

1. Introduction

SeismoSense ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the SeismoSense iOS and Android applications collect, use, and store information.

By using SeismoSense, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the application.

2. Data We Collect

Accelerometer / Sensor Data

SeismoSense reads your device's built-in accelerometer to detect ground motion. This data:

  • Is processed exclusively on your device in real time
  • Is never transmitted to any external server
  • Is stored locally in the app's private storage
  • Is only accessible to you via the in-app Analysis View or CSV export

Location Data (Background Keepalive Only)

SeismoSense requests location permission solely as a technical mechanism to keep the accelerometer active when your screen is locked or the app is in the background. Specifically:

  • Location access is used exclusively as a background keepalive — not to record where you are
  • Location readings are discarded immediately and are never stored, logged, or written to disk
  • No geographic coordinates are attached to seismic event records
  • Location data is never transmitted to any server or third party
  • On iOS, this is implemented via the Zone Monitoring (significant-location-change) API, which requires the "Always" location permission to enable uninterrupted background sensor access
  • You can revoke this permission at any time in Settings → SeismoSense → Location; doing so will disable continuous background monitoring

3. Data We Do NOT Collect

SeismoSense does not collect, store, or transmit the following:

  • Your name, email address, or any personal identifier
  • Account credentials of any kind
  • Device identifiers (IDFA, IDFV, Android Advertising ID)
  • Usage analytics or behavioral tracking data
  • Crash reports (no analytics SDK is embedded)
  • Any seismic or sensor data via network requests
  • Location coordinates — location access is used only as a background keepalive and all readings are discarded immediately

4. On-Device Processing

All signal processing — including the STA/LTA algorithm, bandpass filtering, P-wave and S-wave classification, magnitude estimation, and confidence scoring — runs entirely on your device's CPU. No internet connection is required for or used during seismic analysis.

5. Data Export (CSV)

The app allows you to export your seismic event history as a CSV file. This export is initiated exclusively by you and goes wherever you choose to send it (email, Files app, cloud storage, etc.). SeismoSense does not receive a copy of exported data.

6. Third-Party Services

SeismoSense does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or data collection SDKs. The app makes no network requests related to seismic monitoring.

The app is distributed through Apple's App Store and Google Play Store. Their respective privacy policies govern the download and installation process.

  • Apple Privacy Policy
  • Google Privacy Policy

7. Data Retention & Deletion

All event logs and seismic records are stored in the app's local storage on your device. You can delete all app data at any time by uninstalling the application or clearing app data through your device's settings. We do not retain any copy of your data.

8. Children's Privacy

SeismoSense is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. Because no personal data is collected by the app, there is no associated risk to minors.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected in an updated "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via the app's support page or through the App Store / Google Play listing.

⚠ Important Disclaimer

SeismoSense is a personal scientific instrument for educational and data-logging purposes. It is NOT a certified seismic monitoring system, NOT a government-authorized early warning service, and NOT a substitute for official emergency services or evacuation instructions. Always follow guidance from official emergency authorities during seismic events.
